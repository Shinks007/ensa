Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our affiliate program

Who can join the Ensaasmo affiliate program?

Our affiliate program is open to entrepreneurs, business coaches, content creators, digital marketers, and anyone with an audience interested in entrepreneurial development. We review all applications to ensure alignment with our brand values.

How much can I earn as an affiliate?

You earn 30% commission on every successful referral. For example, if someone purchases our Professional package (₦20,000) through your link, you earn ₦6,000. There's no cap on your earnings – the more you refer, the more you earn.

When and how do I get paid?

We process payments every 14 days for all commissions that have cleared the 14-day refund period. You can choose to receive payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or other available payment methods in your dashboard.

What marketing materials do you provide?

We provide a comprehensive suite of marketing resources including banners, email templates, social media posts, product descriptions, and promotional videos. All materials are professionally designed and regularly updated to maximize conversion rates.

How do you track referrals?

We use advanced tracking technology with 90-day cookies to ensure all your referrals are properly attributed. Our system tracks clicks, conversions, and earnings in real-time, which you can monitor through your affiliate dashboard.

Are there any restrictions on how I can promote Ensaasmo?

While we encourage creativity in your promotional efforts, we prohibit spam, misleading claims, and any activities that could damage our brand reputation. A complete list of guidelines is provided upon approval of your affiliate application.
